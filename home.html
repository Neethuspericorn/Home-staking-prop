STEP 01
Connect Your Wallet

Begin by connecting your Aptos Blockchain wallet to the staking platform. This wallet will be used to hold your claim staked tokens and receive rewards. Ensure your using a supported wallet Pontum, Petra, or Martian Wallets.

STEP 02
Choose Staking Options

Explore the available staking options provided by the platform. This may include different staking durations, reward rates, or other parameters. Assess the risk and rewards associated with each option to make an informed decision.

STEP 03
Select Stake Amount

Decide the amount of PROPS tokens you want to stake. This can vary based on your risk tolerance and investment goals. Once you've chosen your staking amount, initiate the staking process through the platform by confirming the transaction.

STEP 04
Monitor your rewards

Keep track of your staked assets and monitor any changes in rewards. Most staking platforms provide dashboards where you can view your staking status, including current stakes, earned rewards, and other relevant information.

STEP 05
Earn rewards for staking

As your PROPS tokens are staked, you'll start earning rewards over time. These rewards are typically a percentage of your staked amount and are distributed periodically. Be aware of the staking duration and the frequency of payouts.

STEP 06
Claim your Rewards

When you're ready to claim your staking rewards, navigate to the platform's interface and the option to claim rewards. Follow the necessary steps to initiate the reward withdrawal process, and the earned tokens will be deposited into your connected wallet.
